Fort Wayne finally has a Mexican restaurant that knows what it is doing.

Mercado Raised the Bar So High
Everything Else Has To Answer For Itself.

By The Rant Desk | May 2022 | Fort Wayne, IN

We thought we had found the ceiling with Tequila out southwest. Then Mercado showed up and the ceiling moved. The flavor here is sharper, more intentional, and more complex than anything else Fort Wayne's Mexican food scene has produced. You are going to pay more. You are also going to eat food that tastes like someone in the kitchen actually cared about the outcome. That is rarer than it should be in this city.

The Verdict
"The best Mexican restaurant in Fort Wayne. Full stop."

If you have been sleeping on Mercado because of the price point, stop doing that. Go once, eat well, and recalibrate your expectations for what Mexican food in Fort Wayne can actually be.
