Burgers & Brew Wants $18 For a Burger.
We Tried It. Here's the Ugly Truth.

By The Rant Desk | February 2025 | Fort Wayne, IN

Let's be clear about something: an $18 burger in Fort Wayne had better arrive on a golden tray carried by someone in a tuxedo. What we got instead was a dry patty, a brioche bun that disintegrated on contact, and fries that had clearly been sitting under a heat lamp since the previous administration. The ambiance? Loud. The service? Confused. The bill? Offensive.

The Verdict
"You'll spend $60 for two people and leave wishing you'd gone to Taco Bell."

The concept is solid. The execution is not. Fort Wayne has better burgers at half the price. We checked. Multiple times. In the name of journalism.

What makes a good rant

Be specific. "The food was bad" is not a rant. "The pasta tasted like it was cooked in dishwater and seasoned with regret" is a rant.
Be honest. Don't submit a rant because an employee was rude to you personally. We're reviewing food, not people's days.
Be fair. If you have a complaint, you should also be able to say one thing they did right. Even if it's just "the parking was free."
